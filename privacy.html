Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Introduction

WindNow ("we", "our", or "the app") is a weather application designed to provide wind and weather information from various data sources. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Location Data

WindNow requests access to your device's location for the following purposes:

  • Sorting weather stations by distance: When you grant "When In Use" location permission, we use your location to sort nearby weather stations and show distance information.
  • Wind alerts (optional): If you enable wind alerts and grant "Always" location permission, we use your location to determine when you are near monitored weather stations and send you notifications when wind conditions exceed your specified thresholds.

Important: Your location data is processed entirely on your device. We do not transmit, store, or collect your location data on any external servers.

API Keys

If you choose to use Holfuy weather station data, you may enter your own Holfuy API key. This key is stored securely in your device's Keychain and is only used to authenticate requests to the Holfuy service. We do not have access to your API keys.

Weather Data Requests

When you use the app, it makes requests to the following third-party weather data services:

  • Bureau of Meteorology (BoM) - Australian government weather service
  • Holfuy - Weather station network (if configured)
  • Open-Meteo - Weather forecast API

These requests include general location coordinates (latitude/longitude) for the weather stations you've added, not your personal location.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

WindNow displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data for advertising purposes according to their privacy policy. This may include:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • General location (derived from IP)
  • App usage data

For more information about Google's data practices, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device's Settings under Privacy > Advertising.

Weather Data Providers

The weather data providers (BoM, Holfuy, Open-Meteo) may log requests made to their servers. Please refer to their respective privacy policies:

  • Bureau of Meteorology Privacy Policy
  • Holfuy Privacy Policy
  • Open-Meteo Terms

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Your configured weather stations and cards
  • Display preferences and settings
  • Alert configurations
  • Cached weather data

We do not operate any servers that store your personal data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. The only data transmitted from your device is:

  • Weather data requests to the services listed above
  • Advertising-related data to Google AdMob (as described above)

Notifications

If you enable wind alerts, the app will send local push notifications to your device when wind conditions exceed your configured thresholds. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and are not sent through any external notification service.

Children's Privacy

WindNow is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored by the app in your device's settings
  • Delete: Remove the app to delete all locally stored data
  • Opt-out: Disable location services or notifications in your device's Settings
  • Ad preferences: Adjust personalized advertising settings in your device's Privacy settings

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the app's data practices, please contact us through our support page.

Consent

By using WindNow, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
